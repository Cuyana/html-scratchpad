We want to support your in taking proper care of your products & repairing them when needed so that they can last in your closet for as long as possible.

How to Clean

Clean off dirt by wiping down with a dry, soft cloth.

Avoid water contact.

How to Store

To handle our hats, cup the crown instead of holding the brim.

Store in a hat box.

Due to its natural quality, avoid water contact and do not wear our straw hats in the rain as it might lose its shape.
